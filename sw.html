    Handicraft of Brunei Ethnic in Kg Padang Bongawan, Sabah

    ✨ Silverwork and Brasswork ✨

    ❮
    ❯

    The Bruneians in Bongawan village are also famous for their beautiful silver and copper products. There are several types Silverwork in Bongawan Sabah:

    • Jewelry:

      • Crafted silver jewelry, including bracelets, necklaces, earrings, rings, and brooches. These pieces often feature traditional motifs and intricate detailing.

    • Kris (Keris):

      • The kris is a traditional dagger with a wavy blade, often adorned with silver inlay work on the hilt and sheath. It is both a weapon and a symbolic item, representing bravery and honor.
