    Handicraft of Brunei Ethnic in Kg Padang Bongawan, Sabah

    ✨ Basket Weaving ✨

    ❮
    ❯

    Basketry and mat weaving are common crafts among Bruneians in kampung Bongawan Sabah. Artisans use natural materials like bamboo, rattan, and pandan leaves to create functional and decorative items such as baskets, mats, and containers. These items often feature intricate patterns and are used in daily life as well as in ceremonial contexts.

  • Nipah Palm Baskets:

    • The leaves of the nipah palm are also used in basket weaving. These baskets are typically used for practical purposes like carrying rice or other food items. The weaving style tends to be simpler but highly functional.

  • Rattan Baskets:

    • Rattan baskets come in various shapes and sizes, serving multiple purposes such as storage, carrying goods, and decorative items. They often feature intricate weaving patterns.
