    Handicraft of Brunei Ethnic in Kg Padang Bongawan, Sabah

    ✨ Traditional Woven Textile ✨

    ❮
    ❯

    In Bruneian society, traditional woven cloth have multiple roles whose meanings vary according to the situation in which the traditional cloth is utilized. There are several types of traditional woven textiles of the Brunei community in Bongawan Sabah, among them are:

  • Songket:

    • Songket is a luxurious hand-woven fabric made with gold or silver threads. The intricate patterns and designs are created on a loom, and the fabric is often used for special occasions and ceremonial attire.

  • Jong Sarat:

    • Jong Sarat is a traditional Bruneian textile characterized by its rich and elaborate designs. It is woven using silk or cotton threads with supplementary gold or silver threads, creating a brocade effect. Jong Sarat is highly prized and often worn during weddings and other significant cultural events.
